Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, with notable roles including ‘Petruchio’(Taming of the Shrew), ‘Ralph D’(The Motherfu**ker with the Hat), ‘The Messenger’(Euripides’ Medea); and as a new Open Hydrant member, many of their wonderful productions at the amazing Point Theatre. @Tariq.Triano
